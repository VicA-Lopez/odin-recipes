Japanese Curry

Description

A curry that has a thick stew-like consistency with a rich savory taste originating from Japan.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 apple
  • 1.5 lb chicken thighs
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 package curry roux
